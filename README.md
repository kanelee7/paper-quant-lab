# PaperQuantLab
## AI Reasoning Research Workstation

**PaperQuantLab** is a local-first research environment for the longitudinal study of AI analytical styles and reasoning patterns. It provides a technical laboratory for auditing how AI models interpret market regimes through high-fidelity simulation and deterministic temporal replay.

---

### Workstation Identity
PaperQuantLab is a **reasoning observability platform**. It is designed to prioritize analytical depth over execution frequency, serving as a technical bridge between raw market data and structured human interpretation. The workstation provides the necessary primitives to audit, critique, and synthesize AI-generated research findings.

### Research Objectives
- **Reasoning Observability**: A specialized environment for auditing the internal logic of AI decision-making.
- **High-Fidelity Simulation**: A risk-free sandbox for the empirical validation of analytical hypotheses.
- **Replay-Backed Learning**: A system for temporal exploration and evidence-linked post-mortem analysis.
- **Knowledge Synthesis**: A framework for transforming raw experimental data into structured, longitudinal research.

### Strategic Constraints
- **Simulation-Only**: The workstation has no brokerage connectivity and cannot execute live financial transactions.
- **Non-Predictive**: The objective is regime interpretation and analytical style analysis, not market forecasting.
- **Local-First**: All research data, persona histories, and knowledge archives remain on local hardware to ensure privacy.
- **Non-Social**: A focused, private environment for individual or institutional research.

---

### Core Research Workflow

1.  **Experimentation**
    Deploy multiple AI personas into high-fidelity simulated market regimes to observe divergent analytical styles.
2.  **Observation**
    Monitor explainable signals, capturing reasoning narratives and technical indicator snapshots in real-time.
3.  **Deterministic Replay**
    Step back into specific market contexts to audit decision logic with sub-second precision.
4.  **Evidence Synthesis**
    Cluster findings into research insights, linking them to specific historical evidence and replay cases.
5.  **Longitudinal Review**
    Conduct structured post-mortem analysis to validate reasoning reliability and detect behavioral drift.
6.  **Archival**
    Preserve entire research sessions and knowledge bases for long-term comparative study.

---

### Analytical Capabilities

#### Explainable Signal Provenance
Every analytical decision produces a structured `Signal` object. This includes a technical indicator snapshot, a detailed reasoning narrative, and a multi-timeframe reliability score, ensuring full traceability from insight to raw context.

#### Multi-Persona Sandbox
Compare distinct analytical mindsets—such as *Risk-Averse*, *Momentum-Focused*, and *Contrarian*—within identical market contexts. Observe how different reasoning styles navigate regime shifts and interpret conflicting indicators.

#### Deterministic Replay Engine
Reconstruct historical market states with precision. The replay system allows researchers to focus the entire workstation on specific temporal moments, facilitating deep-dive analysis of past AI interpretative behavior.

#### Knowledge Management
Transform transient experimental outcomes into a structured research foundation. Manage a local knowledge base of market patterns, link insights to supporting evidence, and track the evolution of findings over time.

### Workstation Modes

-   **Research Mode**
    The primary interface for active experimentation, real-time monitoring, and signal generation.
-   **Review Mode**
    A focused environment for post-mortem analysis, side-by-side comparison, and evidence synthesis.
-   **Training Mode**
    A guided experience for learning market interpretation and AI reasoning patterns through narrated walkthroughs.

---

### Technical Principles

-   **Data Sovereignty**
    All research assets, including persona logs and knowledge archives, are stored locally.
-   **High-Density Technical Design**
    A charcoal-and-gold interface optimized for cognitive clarity during extended research sessions.
-   **Evidence-Linked Architecture**
    A strict data hierarchy ensuring that every research insight is explicitly linked to its foundational evidence.

---

### Architecture Overview

PaperQuantLab utilizes a modular, service-oriented architecture designed for reliability and reproducibility:

-   **Backend Engine**: Python-based services handling market simulation, persona reasoning (LLM-agnostic), and research state.
-   **Research Workstation**: A React-based frontend utilizing Chakra UI for high-density data visualization and interactive replay.
-   **Persistence Layer**: Local JSON-based archives and metadata management, optimized for versioned research state.

### Installation & Development

#### Prerequisites
- Python 3.10+
- Node.js 18+
- Git

#### Quick Start
1.  **Clone the Repository**
    `git clone https://github.com/user/paper-quant-lab.git`
2.  **Configure Environment**
    Create a `.env` file with necessary API keys for reasoning model providers.
3.  **Initialize Backend**
    `python main.py`
4.  **Initialize Workstation**
    `cd frontend && npm start`
