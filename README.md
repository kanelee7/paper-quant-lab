# PaperQuantLab
### The AI Reasoning Research Workstation

**PaperQuantLab** is a local-first, human-centered research environment designed for studying AI analytical styles, validating reasoning patterns, and accumulating evidence-backed market insights through high-fidelity simulation.

---

## 🏛️ Philosophy & Positioning

PaperQuantLab is a **reasoning observability platform**, not a trading bot. We prioritize **understanding over execution** and **evidence over automated authority**.

- **What it is**: A sophisticated simulation environment for longitudinal research and analytical learning.
- **What it is NOT**: A brokerage-connected platform or a guaranteed-profit "alpha" engine.
- **Simulation-First**: Hard-blocked order execution. All outcomes are recorded within our local research foundation.
- **Local-First**: Your research data, insights, and archives never leave your machine.

## 🚀 Key Research Capabilities

### 🔍 Explainable Outcome Intelligence
Every strategy decision produces a detailed **Signal** object with a clear reasoning trace and a technical indicator snapshot. Performance is tracked across multiple time intervals (5m, 15m, 1h) to validate the longitudinal reliability of the logic.

### 🧠 AI Persona Comparison
Simultaneously run multiple AI personas—each with distinct risk preferences and analytical mindsets—to observe how they interpret conflicting market indicators. Contrast *Conservative*, *Momentum*, and *Contrarian* styles in real-time.

### 🔄 Deterministic Replay Intelligence
Step back into any historical market context. Our replay engine uses saved indicator snapshots to perfectly reproduce the research environment at the time of a decision, supporting rigorous post-mortem analysis.

### 📜 Longitudinal Synthesis & Governance
Cluster research sessions into structured **Narratives** and **Research Reviews**. Audit synthesized findings with automated evidence coverage scoring and provenance tracking from the high-level review down to the raw replay context.

### 📦 Reproducible Archival Workflows
Bundle entire experiments, persona histories, and knowledge bases into portable, versioned archives. Preserve your research state for long-term accumulation and offline analysis.

## 🛠️ Design System

PaperQuantLab features a **premium high-density workstation UI** designed for technical focus:
- **Dark-First Aesthetic**: Optimized for long research sessions.
- **Muted Visual Hierarchy**: Technicalold-gold accents and charcoal surfaces prioritize data clarity.
- **Workflow Modes**: Specialized modes for **Research** (full depth), **Review** (synthesis focus), and **Training** (guided learning).

---

## 🚦 Integrity Mandates

1. **Simulation-Only**: Brokerage execution is strictly prohibited by architecture.
2. **Human-In-The-Loop**: AI is a reasoning assistant; human judgment remains the final authority.
3. **No Hype**: We do not promise "market-beating" returns or autonomous financial success.
