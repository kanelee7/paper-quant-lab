# Project Identity: PaperQuantLab

**PaperQuantLab** is an **AI-assisted multi-asset strategy research sandbox** designed for iterative experimentation and decision analysis.

## Long-Term Direction
The workstation aims to become the primary local-first environment where traders and researchers can:
- Iteratively test and refine market hypotheses.
- Analyze the "Why" behind every strategy signal.
- Replay market scenarios to identify the delta between automated signals and human judgment.
- Use AI reasoning agents to provide diverse perspectives on market conditions.

## AI Research Sandbox
The workstation is a laboratory for **AI Reasoning Comparison**. We treat AI not as a financial authority, but as an experimental persona that can be debated, audited, and compared against other models or historical outcomes.

## Workstation Aesthetic
The workstation follows a **technical-first visual identity**:
- **Dark-First Design**: Optimized for prolonged research and technical focus.
- **High-Density Information**: Prioritizes data depth and evidence visibility over white space.
- **Muted Visual Hierarchy**: Technical old-gold accents and charcoal surfaces ensure the research data remains the primary focal point.
- **Workflow-Aware Interface**: Specialized modes (Research, Review, Training) adapt the workspace ergonomics to the researcher's immediate task.

## Anti-Hype Stance
We explicitly reject the hype-driven narratives of "AI beating the market" or "autonomous wealth generation." This project is grounded in the reality that markets are adversarial and dominated by institutional advantages. Our response is a focus on **Decision Quality** over **Automation Hype**.
