# Research Mindset: Core Philosophy

The fundamental belief of this project is that most retail auto-trading systems fail because they lack transparency and human understanding.

## Core Principles
- **Explainability-First**: If a system cannot explain its reasoning, it should not be making signals.
- **Simulation-First**: All research must be proven in the "safe harbor" of simulation before any discussion of execution.
- **Human-in-the-Loop**: The workstation is a tool for **Decision Support**, not **Decision Replacement**.
- **Iterative Refinement**: Progress is made through tiny, data-driven strategy adjustments and honest post-mortem analysis.

## Anti-Goals
- **No autonomous profit generation**: We do not build "set and forget" bots.
- **No high-frequency trading**: Our focus is on strategic reasoning, not latency advantages.
- **No brokerage automation**: We prioritize the simulated sandbox over live execution interfaces.
- **No cloud infrastructure**: Keep the data, the logic, and the research workstation local and private.
