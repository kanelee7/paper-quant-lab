# Session Handoff: Phase 18 - Interactive Replay Intelligence & Analytical Exploration

## 1. Phase 18 Summary
This phase moved the workstation from "visible information" to **explorable intelligence**. We implemented a suite of interactive tools including a replay timeline scrubber, side-by-side comparative charts, and a replay snapshot system, transforming the platform into a dynamic laboratory for temporal market analysis.

## 2. Key Deliverables

### Replay Timeline Scrubber
- **`ReplayTimeline.tsx`**: A new interactive navigation component.
  - **Temporal Scrubbing**: Researchers can drag a scrubber to move the chart focus through time.
  - **Marker Integration**: Signals are visualized as markers on the timeline for quick jump-navigation.
  - **Evidence Stepping**: Prev/Next buttons allow chronological stepping through research evidence.

### Comparative Replay Workspace
- **Side-by-Side Analysis**: Refactored `App.tsx` to support a twin-chart layout when in **REVIEW** mode.
- **Synchronized Exploration**: Allows researchers to compare different personas or market regimes simultaneously.

### Replay Snapshot System
- **`replay_snapshot_manager.py`**: Backend persistence for saving specific "Replay Moments."
- **`ReplaySnapshotManager.tsx`**: UI for bookmarking analytical peaks, saving contextual notes, and jumping back to saved states.
- **Archive Compatible**: Snapshots are included in research archives, ensuring longitudinal study preservation.

### Interactive Ergonomics & Intelligence
- **Hover Intelligence**: `SignalJournal` rows now provide a reasoning preview and confidence progress bar on hover.
- **Session Evolution**: Added a bar-chart visualization to `QuantInsights` showing win-rate adaptation across the last 8 sessions.
- **Replay Challenge**: Training Mode now includes a "Take Replay Challenge" feature to jump students into significant historical contexts.
- **Motion Polish**: Added slow background drift and pulse animations to the Landing page for a premium technical feel.

## 3. Verified Improvements
- **Exploration Speed**: Replay markers and the scrubber significantly reduce the time needed to navigate large journals.
- **Comparative Depth**: Side-by-side charts allow for direct visual contrast of persona disagreements.
- **Durable Moments**: Researchers can now "save their place" in a complex replay session using the snapshot system.

## 4. Operational Constraints
- **Multi-Chart Performance**: Running two charts simultaneously increases frontend memory usage; performance may vary on lower-end research hardware.
- **Heuristic Evolution**: Cross-session win rates in QuantInsights currently use a simplified aggregate; deep longitudinal filtering is planned for future optimization.
