# Session Handoff: Phase 16 - Frontend Productization & Visual Design Direction

## 1. Phase 16 Summary
This phase transformed PaperQuantLab from a functional research prototype into a **visually polished and productized workstation**. We established a sophisticated dark theme design system, implemented a public-facing landing page concept, and refined the workstation layout to prioritize high-density information hierarchy and technical focus.

## 2. Key Deliverables

### Design System & Visual Identity
- **`theme.ts` Refinement**: Established a "Premium Research" aesthetic.
  - **Palette**: Near-black charcoal background (`background.deep`), slate surfaces (`background.surface`), and muted old-gold accent (`brand.500`).
  - **Typography**: Inter-based sans-serif for UI, JetBrains Mono for technical data.
  - **Components**: Polished buttons, badges, and high-density card layouts that reduce visual noise.

### Product Landing Page
- **`Landing.tsx`**: Created a public-facing entry point.
  - **Value Proposition**: Clearly communicates the platform as an "AI Reasoning Research Workstation."
  - **Integrity Mandates**: Explicitly states "What it is NOT" (no autonomous trading, no profit hype).
  - **Workflow Overview**: Bento-style grid highlighting Signals, Replay, Governance, and Archives.

### Workstation UI Polish
- **Refined Dashboard**:
  - **Mode Switcher**: Improved visibility of Research/Review/Training modes.
  - **Logical Clustering**: Sidebar organized into clear "Active Experimentation," "Knowledge," and "Maintenance" groups.
  - **High-Density Signal Journal**: Refactored for better information density, clearer badging, and improved outcome visibility.
  - **Evidence Provenance**: Enhanced modal reports with backdrop blurs and better visual hierarchy for reasoning traces.

### Public Repository Readiness
- **`README.md`**: Fully rewritten to reflect the platform's research maturity and new design direction.
- **`VISION.md`**: Updated to include the new "Workstation Aesthetic" philosophy.

## 3. Verified Improvements
- **Visual Cohesion**: The entire platform now follows a single, premium design system.
- **Information Hierarchy**: Researchers can more easily navigate between active experiments and synthesized longitudinal reviews.
- **Trustworthy Branding**: The visual tone matches our technical, evidence-first philosophy, avoiding typical "trading hype" aesthetics.

## 4. Operational Constraints
- **Dark-First Only**: The theme is optimized for dark mode; light mode support is secondary and may have visual inconsistencies in some advanced components.
- **Component Density**: The workstation is optimized for large displays (1280px+); some high-density views may require horizontal scrolling on smaller screens.
