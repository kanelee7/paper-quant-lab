# Session Handoff: Phase 19 - Research Knowledge Experience & Guided Interpretation

## 1. Phase 19 Summary
This phase moved the workstation from "explorable information" to **guided understanding**. We implemented a suite of tools designed to help researchers interpret, teach, and internalize accumulated research knowledge. Key additions include a guided replay walkthrough system, progressive disclosure UX patterns, and a critique/reflection layer for human interpretation.

## 2. Key Deliverables

### Guided Replay Walkthrough System
- **`walkthrough_manager.py`**: A new core module that manages structured research narratives.
  - **Narrative Storytelling**: Allows researchers to define step-by-step walkthroughs across historical replay context.
  - **Contextual Annotation**: Every step supports specific notes, regime hints, and automatic temporal navigation.
- **`GuidedWalkthrough.tsx`**: A new sidebar component in the "RESEARCH GUIDANCE" cluster that allows researchers to browse and execute analytical stories.

### Progressive Disclosure & Ergonomics
- **`SignalJournal.tsx` Enhancements**:
  - **Research Reflections**: Added a dedicated reflection layer in the signal detail modal, providing heuristic-based critique prompts.
  - **Adaptive Density**: Implemented progressive disclosure for raw technical indicators, hiding them by default in non-expert modes to reduce cognitive load.
- **`ResearchKnowledgeBase.tsx` Refinement**: Evidence links (supporting signals) are now collapsible, prioritizing high-level synthesis while maintaining evidence provenance.

### Analytical Teaching Foundations
- **Training Mode Extensions**:
  - **Guided Challenges**: Added "Take Replay Challenge" to jump researchers into random significant historical contexts for analytical practice.
  - **Interpretive Hints**: Integrated regime and action hints into the guided review sequence to support observational learning.

### Knowledge Navigation
- **Lightweight Relationship Mapping**: Updated `ComparativeStudyBoard.tsx` to include linked research insights, allowing researchers to navigate between abstract reasoning patterns and specific synthesized findings.

## 3. Verified Improvements
- **Interpretation Speed**: Walkthroughs provide a "curated path" through complex evidence, making longitudinal findings easier to internalize.
- **Reduced Noise**: Progressive disclosure ensures that advanced technical data only surfaces when specifically requested.
- **Deeper Critique**: Heuristic reflection prompts encourage researchers to look beyond the immediate signal and consider historical behavioral drift.

## 4. Operational Constraints
- **Manual Curation**: While the system supports walkthrough execution, the creation of high-quality research narratives remains a human task.
- **Local Persistence**: Walkthroughs are stored locally and are fully compatible with research archives.
