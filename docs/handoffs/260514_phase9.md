# Session Handoff: Phase 9 - Research Knowledge Layer & Insight Synthesis (Final)

## 1. Phase 9 Summary
This phase moved the workstation from "data collection" to **knowledge synthesis**. We implemented a framework to transform raw experimental results into structured research findings, allowing for the accumulation of insights over time.

## 2. Key Deliverables

### Research Insight Framework
- **`insight_manager.py`**: A new core module that manages the lifecycle of `ResearchInsight` objects.
  - **Insight Accumulation**: Allows researchers to synthesize findings (e.g., "Momentum personas overtrade during sideways volatility") and link them to supporting evidence.
  - **Failure Taxonomy**: Implemented a standardized classification system for recurring reasoning issues (e.g., Overconfidence, Volatility Blindness, Trend Chasing).
- **`research_insights.json`**: Local storage for synthesized knowledge and failure classifications.

### Automated Experiment Synthesis
- **Session Summaries**: Added logic to automatically generate summaries for completed research sessions.
  - **Dominant Patterns**: Identifies the most frequent reasoning signatures used during the session.
  - **Strongest Regimes**: Automatically determines which market conditions yielded the best performance.
  - **Drift Observations**: Detects if reasoning remained stable or evolved significantly across the session.
  - **Frequent Failures**: Flags recurring quality issues based on heuristic evaluations.

### Research Memory Dashboard
- **`ResearchKnowledgeBase.tsx`**: A new component for managing synthesized insights and viewing the failure taxonomy.
  - **Evidence Navigation**: Researchers can expand an insight to see linked signals and navigate directly to their **Replay Context**.
- **`SignalJournal.tsx` Enhancements**:
  - **Qualitative Tagging**: Integrated the failure taxonomy into the signal detail modal, allowing researchers to tag signals with standardized failure types during post-mortems.

### Research-Grade UI Updates
- **`ResearchSessionManager.tsx` Enhancements**:
  - **Auto-Summary Modal**: Displays a detailed synthesis report immediately upon completing a research session.
  - **Historical Summary Access**: Researchers can revisit automatic summaries for any past session via the sidebar.

## 3. Verified Improvements
- **Knowledge Accumulation**: Findings are no longer buried in individual sessions but are accumulated in a persistent knowledge layer.
- **Evidence-Linked Insights**: Synthesized findings are directly connected to original signals, ensuring provenance.
- **Pattern Recognition**: Automated summaries help researchers quickly identify analytical blind spots or strengths.
- **Taxonomy-Driven Analysis**: Standardized failure tags enable more precise longitudinal studies of reasoning behavior.

## 4. Operational Constraints
- **Heuristic Synthesis**: Automated summaries are based on frequency analysis of keywords and outcomes; they support, but do not replace, human interpretation.
- **Local-First Design**: All insights and summaries are stored and processed locally.
