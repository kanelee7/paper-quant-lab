# Session Handoff: Phase 12 - Research Governance & Review Reliability (Final)

## 1. Phase 12 Summary
This phase moved the workstation from "narrative synthesis" to **research governance and reliability**. We implemented a robust framework to audit, validate, and verify the trustworthiness of research narratives, ensuring they are backed by sufficient evidence and free from internal contradictions.

## 2. Key Deliverables

### Review Reliability Framework
- **`review_manager.py` Enhancements**:
  - **Evidence Coverage Score**: Heuristic scoring (0-100%) that measures the depth of supporting sessions, insights, and findings.
  - **Diagnostic Reliability Flags**: Automatically identifies weak research areas (e.g., `insufficient_findings`, `no_linked_sessions`).
  - **Contradiction Detection**: Lightweight sentiment-based scanner that identifies conflicting findings across multiple longitudinal reviews for the same market regimes.
  - **Audit Trail Metadata**: Versioned audit tracking for every review, capturing synthesis methods and data source provenance.
  - **Review Updates**: Support for iterative refinement of narratives with automatic audit version incrementing.

### Governance Workspace UI
- **`ResearchReviewBoard.tsx` Refinements**:
  - **Governance & Trust Panel**: Displays coverage scores, reliability flags, and audit versions in the review detail view.
  - **Contradiction Warnings**: Surfaces global alerts when longitudinal conclusions across different reviews conflict.
  - **Provenance Visualization**: Ensures synthesized findings remain navigable back to original replay evidence.

### Reliability & Archival Integration
- **Durable Governance**: All audit and reliability metadata is preserved in research archives, ensuring the governance chain remains intact during transfers or snapshots.

## 3. Verified Improvements
- **Auditable Synthesis**: Researchers now have a quantitative measure of how well-supported their longitudinal findings are.
- **Cross-Review Awareness**: The system detects when new research contradicts older findings, encouraging deeper analysis of behavioral drift.
- **Narrative Provenance**: Strengthened the link between high-level synthesis and raw evidence, ensuring every claim is verifiable.

## 4. Operational Constraints
- **Heuristic Scanning**: Contradiction detection uses keyword-based sentiment analysis; it is intended to flag potential issues for human review, not to provide definitive truth.
- **Local-Only Governance**: All reliability checks are performed against local JSON datasets.
