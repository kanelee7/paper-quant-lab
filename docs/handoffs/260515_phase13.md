# Session Handoff: Phase 13 - Research Workflow Ergonomics & Training Mode Foundations

## 1. Phase 13 Summary
This phase improved the operational clarity and usability of the workstation by introducing **Workspace Modes** and reorganizing the UI into logical clusters. We also established the foundations for **Training Mode**, a learning-oriented environment that provides guided reasoning explanations and market context hints without weakening the core research integrity model.

## 2. Key Deliverables

### Workspace Modes Foundation
- **Mode Switcher**: A global state in the dashboard allows switching between:
  - **RESEARCH**: Full access to all tools, including automation and diagnostics.
  - **REVIEW**: Focused on longitudinal synthesis, insights, and evidence review (hides active trading controls).
  - **TRAINING**: Guided learning focus, providing simplified explanations and market regime hints.
- **Component Adaptability**: Components like `SignalJournal` and `PersonaSandbox` now adjust their interface and level of detail based on the active mode.

### Workflow Ergonomics
- **Logical Sidebar Clusters**: The sidebar is now organized into three distinct sections to reduce cognitive overload:
  - **ACTIVE EXPERIMENTATION**: Real-time tools (Exchange, Sessions, Persona Sandbox).
  - **RESEARCH KNOWLEDGE**: Synthesis tools (Insights, Reviews, Quant Stats).
  - **MAINTENANCE & RELIABILITY**: Dataset health tools (Diagnostics, Archives, Performance).
- **Navigation Clarity**: Improved labels, tooltips, and color-coded mode indicators throughout the UI.

### Training & Learning Foundations
- **Guided Explanations**: The `SignalJournal` now provides:
  - **Regime Hints**: Explanations of market conditions (e.g., Trending vs. Volatile) and their impact on strategies.
  - **Action Hints**: Simple descriptions of AI decisions (Buy/Sell/Hold).
- **Persona Guidance**: `PersonaSandbox` now includes "Persona Hints" to explain the analytical style of different AI brains (e.g., "Contrarian: Looking for tops and bottoms").
- **Replay Learning**: Improved visibility for bookmarked signals to support targeted case studies.

## 3. Verified Improvements
- **Reduced Cognitive Overload**: Reorganizing the sidebar makes the workstation feel more approachable.
- **Improved Discoverability**: Mode-based filtering ensures researchers see the most relevant tools for their current task.
- **Educational Value**: Training mode foundations provide context for why AI models reason in specific ways, supporting human analytical learning.

## 4. Operational Constraints
- **Simulation-Only**: All learning and training workflows remain simulation-first; no authority is given to AI models for financial execution.
- **Provenance Preservation**: Ergonomics improvements are UI-level; full evidence provenance and governance data remain accessible to expert researchers.
